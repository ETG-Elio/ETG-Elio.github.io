ETG - Elio’s Training Grounds.

We are a dedicated community for VRFS, creating arenas and maps in the Virtual Reality game VRFS. All of our maps are completely free—no credits or hidden costs. The original creator and founder of this initiative is Elio (elio63091 on Discord).

Our mission is to help everyone improve their skills in VRFS, providing opportunities for growth and progression that might otherwise be hard to achieve. Join us to level up your gameplay and enjoy a community focused on skill, collaboration, and excellence.
